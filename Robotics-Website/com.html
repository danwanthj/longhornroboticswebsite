AMAs with Local Schools

In the past, one of our main forms of outreach was to host AMAs (Ask me Anythings) at local middle and elementary schools. We would bring 4-5 members as well as experts in various engineering professions for students to get their questions about engineering and robotics answered.

Dartmouth Makerspace

Dartmouth is a local middle school near Leigh High School. Many of Dartmouth's students come to Leigh yearning to join Longhorn Robotics, so we aim to give them a headstart. From 2019-2022 we have held a program collaborating with Dartmouth's Makerspace to teach robotics to all those who are interested.

Python Coding Workshop

Over the course of a month during the COVID season, we collaborated with a VEX robotics team at a neighboring high school–Branham High School–to bring forth an online learning environment where students would learnn Python then make their own projects. Students were given a large amount of knowledge in Python, in which they could use to develop further, more advanced projects.
