FIRST® Tech Challenge

Each year, teams of high school students work during a six-week period to build robots capable of competing in that year’s game. Robots complete tasks such as placing balls and blocks into goals, placing cones onto poles, shooting rings into goals, and stacking blocks up to 20 high! The game, along with the required set of tasks, changes annually. Click the button below to learn more!
